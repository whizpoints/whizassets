## The Intersection of Telecommunications & Software Engineering

Many ask how **Telecommunication Engineering** connects directly with software engineering. In practice, the two disciplines complement each other:

1. **Low-Level Protocols & Networks:** Understanding TCP/IP models, socket management, and networking constraints allows me to construct applications resilient to network latency and data loss.
2. **Real-Time Data Pipelines:** Telecommunications principles regarding data streaming directly inform real-time system architectures, WebSocket microservices, and live inventory sync engines.
3. **Hardware-Software Integration:** From microcontrollers and IoT hardware to receipt printers and barcode readers, my background bridges physical devices with cloud software infrastructure.

## Featured Projects Developed at Whizpoint

At Whizpoint Solutions, software quality is measured by production utility. Explore all active case studies at the official **[Whizpoint Projects Showcase](https://whizpoint.app/projects)**.

Key projects designed and deployed under my direction:

### 1. [WhizPOS — Enterprise Point of Sale System](https://whizpoint.app/projects)
An offline-first, scalable retail management platform designed for inventory synchronization, automated M-Pesa reconciliation, and real-time transaction logging.

### 2. [M-Pesa Daraja Middleware & Webhook Service](https://whizpoint.app/projects)
A high-throughput payment middleware engineered for C2B and B2C M-Pesa transactions, providing immediate callback processing and internal ledger mapping.

### 3. [WhatsApp Service Automation & Microservices](https://whizpoint.app/projects)
Custom Node.js microservices that connect WhatsApp communication channels directly with business databases for order handling, customer notifications, and status monitoring.
